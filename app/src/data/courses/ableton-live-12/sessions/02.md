## Phase 1 — Learn (40 min)

- Live 12's Scale Mode: enable it in the MIDI clip, choose C Minor. Only in-key notes are highlighted.
- Piano Roll: draw mode (pencil), select mode (arrow), velocity lane at the bottom.
- Load Wavetable or a stock piano preset. Draw 8 notes — stay in the highlighted rows.

## Phase 2 — Do (65 min)

- Write a simple 8-bar melody clip in Session View. Vary lengths — some short, some long.
- Write a second clip with a variation of the same melody. Now you have two scenes.
- Add it to your drum track from Session 1. Launch both. Listen to how they sit together.

## 15-Min Review

Hum your melody back. If you can't hum it, it's too complex. Simplify to 4–5 notes maximum. The best LoFi melodies are often just 3 notes rearranged.
