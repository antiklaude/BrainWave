## Phase 1 — Learn (40 min)

- Electronic music fundamentals: subtractive synthesis — oscillator → filter → amp envelope.
- In Wavetable: set Oscillator 1 to a sawtooth, enable Unison (4 voices, ~10 cents detune).
- Filter section: low-pass filter, cutoff controls brightness, resonance adds character.
- LFO routed to filter cutoff = automatic wobble. Assign in the modulation matrix.

## Phase 2 — Do (65 min)

- Build 3 distinct sounds from scratch in Wavetable: a pad, a pluck, and a bass.
- Pad: slow attack (1s), medium decay, long release. Heavy reverb send.
- Pluck: fast attack, short decay. Map LFO to filter for movement.
- Bass: sine wave, short attack, medium release. Side-chain to kick.
- Layer all 3 into a 4-bar loop. Export.

## 15-Min Review

Solo each sound. Does each one have a distinct role — low, mid, high? They shouldn't fight each other. If the pad and the pluck clash, high-pass the pad above 400Hz to carve out space.
