## Phase 1 — Learn (40 min)

- EDM: 128 BPM. Supersaw = Wavetable with Unison set to 8 voices, detune at ~15 cents, stereo spread wide.
- Auto Filter on the lead: automate the cutoff from 200Hz closed to full open over 8 bars — that IS the buildup.
- Drop anatomy: Auto Filter closes → build for 8 bars → one beat of silence → Auto Filter snaps fully open → drop.

## Phase 2 — Do (65 min)

- 128 BPM. 4/4 kick on every beat. Off-beat open hi-hat. Clap on 2 and 4.
- 4-chord progression + supersaw lead on top with Auto Filter cutoff automation.
- Arrange: 8-bar buildup → 16-bar drop → 8-bar breakdown → 16-bar drop again. Export.

## 15-Min Review

Find a reference EDM track in the same style. Play yours back to back with it at matched volume. Where does yours feel thin compared to the reference? That gap is your next thing to fix — usually the low end or the reverb size on the lead.
