## Phase 1 — Learn (45 min)

- EQ Eight: drag it onto a track. High-pass (filter 1) everything except kick/bass at 60–80Hz.
- Notch out the mud: boost 200–400Hz to find it, then cut to remove it.
- Compressor on drum bus: 4:1 ratio, 10ms attack, 60ms release. Watch the gain reduction meter — aim for 3–6dB of GR.

## Phase 2 — Do (60 min)

- Open your LoFi track. Add EQ Eight to every track. High-pass first on all non-bass elements.
- Group your drums (Cmd+G). Put a Compressor on the drum group. Compare bypass vs on.
- Use the Utility device on the master: set low frequencies to mono below 80Hz.

## 15-Min Review

Solo each track one at a time. Does anything sound harsh or boxy on its own? Find the offensive frequency by boosting EQ Eight and sweeping until it hurts, then cut that frequency. This is how pros EQ.
